BOOK 1 IN THE SERIES

Sathya Sai Parenting
Part 1

by Rita Bruce

These teachings are for students, young adults, married couples, parents, and grandparents. They focus on the Restoration of the Family Home — repairing the sacred structure of family life that has been worn by the strong winds of changing values.

This book serves as a practical instruction manual to create the right environment for children to grow with character, compassion, and spiritual values rooted in Sathya Sai Baba’s teachings.

CORE PURPOSE

"This book is an instruction manual to create the right environment for children."

THE AUTHOR

Rita Bruce

with Robert Bruce

Rita and Robert Bruce have been devotees of Sri Sathya Sai Baba and active members in the Sai Organization for over 45 years. They have been married over 60 years and raised 4 children. Rita is the author of five books personally named by Sathya Sai Baba: Vision of Sai I & II, Sathya Sai Parenting I & II, and Love of Conscience. Together they have traveled the world sharing these teachings through seminars and public talks.
